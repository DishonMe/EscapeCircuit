\section*{Alice and Bob vs Casino Challenge}

\subsection*{Objective}
Alice and Bob want to beat the Casino in a strange 7-round guessing game.

Alice and Bob are allowed to plan together before the game starts. Bob sneaks into the Casino and learns the Casino's future bits, but after that he can no longer communicate with Alice. So the only thing Alice can rely on is the circuit they designed ahead of time.

Your job is to design Alice's circuit so that this pre-planned strategy gives them an advantage across the whole match.

\subsection*{The Game}
Now for the formal rules.

The match has exactly 7 rounds. In each round, three bits are involved:
\begin{itemize}
  \item $A$ is Alice's bit, produced by your circuit,
  \item $B$ is Bob's bit,
  \item $C$ is the Casino's bit.
\end{itemize}

Bob knows the full Casino sequence ahead of time and may choose his own bit stream before the game begins. Alice cannot react verbally during play, so her behavior must be encoded entirely in the circuit.

\subsection*{Timing Model}
The validator runs exactly 7 clock cycles, and the circuit is reset before each test, so all DFF state starts at 0.

On round $i$, the circuit receives the current Bob bit $B_i$ and casino bit $C_i$ and emits Alice's bit $A_i$.
Because the design is sequential, the information from round $i$ may also be stored and reused on round $i+1$.

This makes the puzzle more than a single combinational decision. The circuit may remember earlier rounds, build internal state, and use that state later in the match.

\subsection*{Winning Condition}
A round is a win only when all three bits match:
\[
A_i = B_i = C_i
\]

The goal is to build Alice so that, for every Casino sequence, there exists a Bob strategy that wins at least 4 out of the 7 rounds.

In other words, the pair wants a strategy that wins the majority of the match, even though Alice never sees the Casino directly and Bob cannot talk to her once the game begins.

\subsection*{Interface}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Inputs: $B$, $C$
  \item Output: $A$
\end{itemize}
